Welcome to our webpage! We understand that some of you may not know where to go, so here is a quick guide. In the top right of the webpage, you will see a navigation bar, you can use this to navigate the website. If you dont know what the gathering is, read on as we will shortly explain it.

Our goal in the gathering is to preach to like minded people about the importance of going on missions for christ, and we want to lead aspiring pastors onto the journey towards their first mission. We usually meet on Thursdays from 6:30 to 9(including time to eat!). For more Information, click on Calender in the top right.
